Parallel Search now speaks French

Version 1.0.3 is especially for French-speaking users

Parallel Search Deluxe 1.0.3 has just been released, and the app has now been completely localized for the French Language.

published on 19 Jul 2021 in

Parallel Search Deluxe 1.0.3 has just been released, and the app has now been completely localized for the French Language. All of the menus, buttons, labels, and strings are now also in French.

Parallel Search Deluxe 1.0.3 has just been released, and the app has now been completely localized for the French Language. All of the menus, buttons, labels, and strings are now also in French.

Parallel Search detects the user’s preferred language from their own System Preferences.

Furthermore, the search engine base URLs are replaced with French equivalents such as https://fr.search.yahoo.com for Yahoo and www.google.fr for Google  to serve French speaking users better.

Parallel Search is available for download on the Mac App Store .
